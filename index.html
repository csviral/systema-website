What Is Systema?

Systema is a practical training method focused on breathing, movement, awareness, adaptability, and remaining calm under pressure. Training develops resilience, mobility, confidence, and the ability to respond naturally to challenges in daily life.

Four Core Principles

Breath

Control your breath. Control your state.

Structure

Strong, relaxed, efficient movement.

Timing

The right action at the right moment.

Awareness

See clearly. Stay present. Stay free.
